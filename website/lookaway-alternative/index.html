A LookAway-style alternative built for Windows.

If you like the idea of scheduled full-screen eye breaks but need a Windows-first option, GazeOff offers a calm tray-based workflow with automatic pause detection and adjustable reminders.

Choose based on workflow, not just a timer

Break apps can share the same basic goal while feeling very different in daily use. GazeOff is designed around Windows tray controls, configurable short and long breaks, blink reminders, posture nudges, and Smart Pause behavior for meetings, fullscreen video, games, and time away.

Why people look for an alternative

You may need native Windows distribution, different timing controls, local statistics, or reminders that wait during meetings and entertainment. GazeOff is available through Microsoft Store, receives Windows-managed updates, and keeps its activity data on your PC.

Try the default rhythm, then make it yours

The default schedule uses 20 minutes of work, a 25-second short break, and a three-minute recovery break after five short cycles. You can change those values, disable individual reminders, choose how breaks look, and decide how quickly inactivity counts as a real break.
